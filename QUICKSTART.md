## 🔄 Switching Between Roles

To switch roles:
1. Go to any page (e.g., `/alumni/dashboard`)
2. Click your **Profile** → **Logout**
3. Login again
4. You can choose a different role at `/role-selection`
5. Or navigate directly:
   - Alumni: `/alumni/dashboard`
   - Admin: `/admin/dashboard`
